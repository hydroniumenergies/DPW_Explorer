Why this now works: Carbonate crossover flux can never be driven to zero by pressure alone — it is dominated by electromigration. Instead, Δp_min is correctly defined as the pressure that suppresses interfacial [CO₃²⁻] to a chosen fraction of its ambient value (the suppression target slider), preventing GDL pore precipitation. The pore diameter slider controls P_flood (ceiling). Smaller pores = higher ceiling = wider window.
